My Experience in the Industry

With over 10 years of experience in the graphic design industry, I’ve had the privilege of working on a wide range of projects, from branding and logo design to digital and print media. My journey has been a blend of both commercial work with established companies and self-employed projects, allowing me to hone my skills and develop a unique creative approach.

Commercial Experience

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with numerous businesses, ranging from startups to well-known brands. As part of a commercial design team, I contributed to creating impactful visual identities, digital campaigns, and promotional materials that helped businesses engage with their target audience and strengthen their brand presence.

Some of the highlights of my commercial experience include:

  • Branding & Identity Development: Designing logos, brand guidelines, and marketing assets for businesses in various sectors, ensuring their visual identity aligns with their values and goals.

  • Marketing Collateral: Creating brochures, flyers, and advertisements that effectively communicate a business's message and attract attention in competitive markets.

  • Web Design: Collaborating with developers to design and launch visually compelling websites that are user-friendly, responsive, and optimized for performance.

Self-Employed Experience

As a self-employed graphic designer, I have had the freedom to collaborate directly with clients, taking on a wide range of projects that challenge me creatively. From crafting bespoke logo designs to creating full brand identities, my work as a freelancer has allowed me to build a diverse portfolio that reflects my adaptability and passion for design.

Some of the services I offer as a self-employed designer include:

  • Custom Logo Design: Tailored logos that capture the essence of a business and leave a lasting impression on its audience.

  • Branding Packages: Full branding solutions, including color schemes, typography, and marketing materials, to help businesses build a strong and cohesive visual identity.

  • Print & Digital Media: Designing promotional materials, social media graphics, and website assets that are on-brand and visually appealing.

Why My Experience Matters

Over the years, I’ve developed a keen understanding of design principles, marketing strategies, and the importance of user experience. Whether I’m working on a commercial project or collaborating with clients as a freelancer, I bring a wealth of knowledge and a deep passion for design to every job. I am always striving to improve my craft, keep up with industry trends, and deliver exceptional results for my clients.

I’m excited to continue growing and taking on new challenges in the design world. Whether you’re looking for a fresh look for your business or need creative solutions to solve complex design problems, I’d love to help bring your vision to life.
