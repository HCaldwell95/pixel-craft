About Us

At Pixel Craft, we are a team of passionate designers dedicated to helping brands communicate their vision through creative and innovative design solutions.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality designs that resonate with your audience and elevate your brand. We believe in a collaborative approach, working closely with you to bring your ideas to life.

Our ethos is rooted in creativity, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.


Based in Abergele, we are surrounded by the stunning landscapes of North Wales, which inspire our creativity and passion. This charming coastal town offers a unique blend of natural beauty, rich history, and a strong sense of community.

We chose Abergele as the home of our business because it reflects our values of quality, authenticity, and personal connections. Being part of such a welcoming town allows us to foster meaningful relationships with our clients while contributing to the local community.
